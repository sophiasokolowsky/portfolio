Welcome!

My name is Sophia Sokolowsky. I am an officer in the United States Air Force and a developer dedicated to bridging the gap between design and security . My work focuses on building intuitive, secure systems that prioritize both usability and protection. I specialize in creating user-centered digital experiences that are as secure as they are seamless.

My Projects

Air Force Holm Center Portal Caset Study & Redesign

A redesign of the U.S. Air Force Holm Center's homepage focused on improving accessibility, usability, and mobile compatibility. I restructured the site's architecture by improving its visual hierarchy, created mockups for desktop, tablet, and mobile user views, and coded a homepage prototype with fully responsive elements, providing a more intuitive user experience.

Laundry Interface Case Study & Redesign

This project explores how improving everyday system design - in this case, Brown University's dorm laundry interface - can strengthen user trust, security, and operational efficiency. By identifying usability flaws and addressing points of user frustration, I focused on how thoughtful redesigns promote better system compliance and resilience, both of which are foundational to cybersecurity practices in critical environments like the Air Force.

Case Study on Accessible Design & System Resilience

This project analyzes critical components of everyday digital interfaces to identify how input methods, output behavior, and component states affect accessibility, usability, and user trust. By ensuring that systems are intuitive and inclusive across all user interactions, I demonstrated how intentional design can strengthen compliance and therefore security.

Secure Learning Platform Design

This project focused on designing an intuitive, secure learning platform for Miyagi Labs based solely on a startup concept description. Through iterative prototyping, critique, and user testing, we explored how system clarity, accessibility, and component consistency drive user trust and operational success.
